Ambassadors for Christ Outreach Ministry

  • Who We Are

    The Ambassadors 4 Christ Outreach Ministry is a faith-based evangelistic outreach ministry established in the USA in 2018 by Min. Flora Joseph-Francois. We put our Christian faith in action by partnering with local churches in the United States and two Christian ministries ("Lamentation" and "Maranatha Groups") in Haiti for the purpose of sharing the gospel through regular Bible study, prayer, fellowship, feeding, clothing and life skills training of the unreached and the poor communities of men, women and children in the nation of Haiti. Our desire is to help Haitians become all that God intends them to be.

  • Our Vision

    Our goal is to serve and restore hope to the most vulnerable people of Haiti through the teachings of the gospel of Jesus Christ and through outreach programs that meet physical, social and mental needs in order to restore hope, empower and promote self-sufficiency.
